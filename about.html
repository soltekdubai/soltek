About SolTek

Who We Are

At SolTek, we believe in a future powered by sustainable, renewable energy. Founded by industry professionals with experience in solar energy solutions, we specialize in field service support, maintenance, and consultation for solar power systems. We’re dedicated to helping businesses and homeowners transition to cleaner, more efficient energy solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities with reliable solar energy technology and support, making renewable energy more accessible and sustainable. We aim to drive innovation in solar energy services across the UAE, Indonesia, and beyond.

Why Choose Us

With years of experience in the solar energy sector, including troubleshooting complex network issues, we offer unmatched expertise. Our founder’s background in working with companies like Tesla gives us an edge in understanding the technical intricacies of solar energy systems, making us the go-to experts for maintenance and technical support.

Values & Vision

  • Innovation: We’re committed to staying ahead of the curve with cutting-edge solutions in solar energy.
  • Sustainability: We’re passionate about creating a cleaner, greener future for the planet.
  • Integrity: Our focus is on providing honest, reliable service to all of our clients.
  • Global Impact: We believe in making a positive impact globally, starting with sustainable energy solutions.
